Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data for the PromptVault app.

Delete your account (fastest)

  1. Open the PromptVault app on your Android device.
  2. Go to About → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and prompts will be scheduled for deletion.

If the option isn’t visible yet or you cannot access the app, use the email request below.

Request via Email Contact support

What we delete

  • Your Firebase Authentication user (Google Sign‑In).
  • All prompt data you created in PromptVault (stored under your UID).
  • Associated metadata (tags, titles, timestamps, collections).

What we may keep temporarily

  • Minimal security and operational logs for up to 30 days for abuse prevention and legal compliance.

We do not sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Identity verification

For email requests, contact us from the same Google account you used to sign in. We may ask for your in‑app UID to ensure we delete the correct account.

Timeline

  • App-initiated: Immediate for active records; backups/logs within 30 days.
  • Email request: Acknowledgement in 48 hours; completion within 7 days.
